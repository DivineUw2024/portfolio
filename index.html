SkillConnect Rwanda

Connecting skilled workers with opportunities

SkillConnect Rwanda addresses a critical gap in the Rwandan job market by creating a platform specifically designed for TVET graduates and skilled tradespeople. Unlike traditional job platforms that rely on CVs and formal qualifications, this platform allows skilled workers to showcase their capabilities through visual portfolios, making their expertise immediately apparent to potential employers.

Core Features

Visual portfolio system with photo and video uploads
Location-based job matching and search
Direct messaging between workers and employers
Rating and review system for quality assurance
Mobile-responsive design for accessibility
Advanced filtering by skills, location, and availability
